My Story

Since completion of my Bachelor of Science in Communicative Disorders degree, I have had the privilege of working primarily in the school sector, providing assessment and intervention support to children presenting with a variety of speech, language and literacy delays/disorders.

In addition, I have had the fortunate experience of working at NONA Child Development Centre with preschoolers and in the private sector working primarily with pre-schoolers.

I have a strong personal commitment to professional development and am continually seeking new and exciting learning opportunities. Wondering about my background with a certain communication difference or disorder? Feel free to contact me to discuss my education and experience, ask questions, or learn more about what I offer.
