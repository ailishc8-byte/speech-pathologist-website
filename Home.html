Crowne Speech Therapy for Toddlers and Kids

Speech Sound Disorders

Speech sound disorders in children encompasses a range of difficulties related to the production of speech sounds. Articulation disorders occur when individual sounds are not formed correctly. Phonological impairment involves more numerous errors due to a limited consonant and vowel repertoire with frequent omission of sounds.

How I can Help

Sounds are targeted to enhance clarity and to improve outcomes of reading and writing instruction. Patterns of errors are targeted to improve intelligibility.
